Overall, I'll Always Be A Student

I immigrated to the United States at the age of 5 and I have been in the Bay Area ever since. I graduated from San Francisco State University with a degree in Biology concentrated in Physiology. I got my first full time job working at Passport Capital, an investment firm, as the assistant to the General Counsel, as well as prior to that I worked at a personal injury law firm. Originally, I wanted to pursue a career in healthcare or law.

But then I was introduced to Lambda School and decided to go for a career change. I knew learning how to code in Silicon Valley would create more opportunities for me and finding a job I would be happy doing. And that is how I got back to being a student again.
